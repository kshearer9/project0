Light

Agave plants require very bright light throughout the year so keep it on the windowsill to give it lots of direct sunlight.

Fertilising your Agave

Fertilising Agaves is not needed but it will stimulate growth. You can use an all purpose liquid fertiliser, diluted to half of the recommended strength once a month only during late Spring and Summer when your plant is growing.
